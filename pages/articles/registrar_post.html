Dear Students,

We hope this message finds you well. We would like to inform you that the grades for the recently completed semester are now ready and available for viewing. We appreciate your patience throughout the grading process.

To access your grades, please follow the steps below:

  1. Visit our school's official website at [website URL].
  2. Log in to your student portal using your username and password. If you have forgotten your login credentials, please contact the Registrar's Office for assistance.
  3. Once logged in, navigate to the "Grades" or "Academic Records" section.
  4. You will find a list of your courses for the semester and the corresponding grades you have received.

Take the time to review your grades thoroughly. If you have any questions or concerns regarding a specific grade, please reach out to the respective course instructor or the Registrar's Office.

We would like to remind you that grades reflect your academic performance and serve as an important indicator of your progress.
